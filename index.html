Who We Are

Your Trusted Lubricant Partner

Based in Aba, Nigeria — serving wholesale and retail customers with premium quality oils.

100%
Authentic Products

COTTO Enterprise is a premier lubricant oil trading company headquartered at 63 Jubilee Road, Aba, Abia State. We specialize in wholesale and retail supply of high-quality lubricants to workshops, fleets, industries, and individuals.

We proudly produce our own COTTO brand — engine oils, ATF, hydraulic oils, gear oils, greases, and filters — formulated to meet international standards. Alongside COTTO, we stock global leaders like Mobil, Total, Castrol, Ammasco, Seahorse and more.
